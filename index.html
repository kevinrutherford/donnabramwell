This is an invitation to take part in my PhD research...

EXPLORING THE ROLE OF EMPLOYERS AND MANAGERS
IN SUPPORTING PEOPLE WITH LONG-TERM CONDITIONS
IN THE WORKPLACE

I would be grateful if you would consider helping me with my research project on people's experience of long-term illness (or long-term conditions) at work. I am a PhD student in the Health Sciences Group - Primary Care at the University of Manchester, carrying out this research for my thesis.

The number of people living with a long-term condition is set to rise in the coming years, which will mean that more people with long-term illness will be employed in the labour market. In light of this, we are interested in exploring your experiences, views and opinions about supporting those with long-term conditions in the workplace. Currently there is little research in this area and this study provides an opportunity for employers and/or line managers to air their views.

Click this button to download a PDF Information Sheet which explains more about this project and what your involvement in the research would be so that you can decide whether or not you wish you to participate. If you would like to take part in this research and help us to understand your views of supporting long-term conditions in the workplace then please contact me at the University of Manchester by clicking the button below my photo.

Thank you very much for reading this letter and for considering taking part in the study. I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely,
